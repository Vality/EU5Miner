# Code of Conduct

EU5Miner is a small technical project and expects collaboration to stay respectful, specific, and useful.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep reports and review feedback specific, reproducible, and technical.
- Assume good faith and stay open to correction.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome contact.
- Publishing private information or suspected security details publicly.
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately derailing project work.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project-managed spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct problems privately to the maintainer through GitHub and include links or screenshots when relevant.

Security reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of this process.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove content and may limit participation when behavior violates this code.
